# Rights and reuse boundary

## Audio

The parent audio was obtained from the LibriVox catalog page for *The Tao Teh King*, read by Eric S. Piotrowski. LibriVox states that its recordings are public domain in the United States and tells users outside the United States to verify their local legal position.

This package therefore does **not** label the recording as CC-licensed or universally public domain.

```text
AUDIO_RIGHTS_LABEL = LIBRIVOX_PUBLIC_DOMAIN_US_CLAIM
NON_US_REUSE_STATUS = NOT_INDEPENDENTLY_VERIFIED
EXCHANGE_SCOPE = PRIVATE_EVAL_LABS_COMPARISON
PUBLIC_REDISTRIBUTION_AUTHORITY = NOT_CLAIMED
```

## Text

The reference text is the James Legge translation published in 1891 and distributed by Project Gutenberg. The package records the exact source and does not claim rights in editorial website presentation.

## Selected WAV

The selected WAV is a derivative export made from the parent MP3. The export does not create new permission over the underlying performance.
